Home Services Ghana - Data Deletion Request

We respect your right to data privacy

⚠️ Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please review what will be deleted:

  • Your account profile and personal information
  • Service booking history and preferences
  • Payment history and saved payment methods
  • Messages and communication history
  • Reviews and ratings given
  • Saved addresses and locations

📋 Account Deletion Process

Step 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

Delete your account directly from the Home Services Ghana app:

  1. Open the Home Services Ghana app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Scroll down to "Account Management"
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your decision
  6. Your account will be deleted within 24 hours

Step 2: Email Request (Alternative)

If you cannot access the app, email us directly:

  • Send to: privacy@homelinkgh.com
  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include: Your registered email address or phone number

⏱️ Deletion Timeline

  • Immediate: Account deactivated and login disabled
  • Within 24 hours: Personal data removed from active systems
  • Within 30 days: Complete removal from backup systems
  • Legal retention: Some data may be retained as required by Ghana law

🔄 Data Deletion Options

Full Account Deletion:

  • Complete account removal
  • All personal data deleted
  • Service history removed
  • Cannot be undone

Partial Data Deletion:

You can also request deletion of specific data while keeping your account:

  • Booking history only
  • Saved addresses only
  • Payment methods only
  • Chat message history only

Email us specifying which data you want deleted.
