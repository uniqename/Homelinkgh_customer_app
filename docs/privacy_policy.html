Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2024
Effective Date: December 28, 2024
Ghana Data Protection Act Compliance: This privacy policy is fully compliant with Ghana's Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843) and international privacy standards.

1. Introduction

HomeLinkGH Limited ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and services.

HomeLinkGH is Ghana's premier home services platform that connects customers with verified service providers across Ghana, with special features for diaspora connectivity.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, profile photo
  • Identity Verification: Ghana Card number, government-issued ID (for providers)
  • Contact Details: Physical address, emergency contacts
  • Professional Information: Skills, experience, certifications (for providers)

2.2 Location Information

  • GPS Location: Real-time location during service delivery
  • Service Addresses: Locations where services are requested or provided
  • Geographic Data: General location for provider matching and service availability

2.3 Payment Information

  • Payment Methods: Credit/debit card information (processed via PayStack)
  • Mobile Money: Mobile money account details for Ghana-based payments
  • Transaction History: Records of payments, bookings, and service history

2.4 Usage Data

  • App Usage: Features used, time spent, navigation patterns
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Communication: Messages between customers and providers

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

  • Connect customers with appropriate service providers
  • Process bookings and facilitate service delivery
  • Enable real-time tracking and communication
  • Process payments securely through PayStack

3.2 Safety and Verification

  • Verify provider identities using Ghana Card validation
  • Conduct background checks for service providers
  • Monitor service quality and user safety
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security

3.3 Platform Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality
  • Personalize user experience and recommendations
  • Develop new features and services
  • Provide customer support and assistance

4. Location Services

Why We Use Location:

  • Provider Discovery: Find nearby service providers
  • Service Delivery: Real-time tracking during active services
  • Safety: Emergency assistance and service verification
  • Diaspora Services: Enable international booking to Ghana locations
Your Control: You can disable location services in your device settings, but this may limit app functionality. Location data is only collected during active app use and service delivery.

5. Information Sharing

5.1 With Service Providers

We share necessary information with service providers to facilitate bookings:

  • Customer name and contact information
  • Service location and requirements
  • Booking details and special instructions

5.2 With Payment Processors

Payment information is processed by PayStack, Ghana's leading payment processor, in compliance with PCI DSS standards.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by Ghana law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect user safety and platform security
  • Investigate fraud or policy violations

6. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure Storage: Personal data is encrypted at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Payment Security: PCI DSS compliant payment processing

7. Your Rights (Ghana Data Protection Act)

Under Ghana's Data Protection Act, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing activities
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for data processing
Exercise Your Rights: Contact us at privacy@homelinkgh.com to exercise any of these rights.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information for different periods:

  • Active Accounts: While your account remains active
  • Transaction Records: 7 years (Ghana tax and business requirements)
  • Safety Records: 3 years for provider verification and safety
  • Marketing Data: Until you opt out or withdraw consent

9. International Data Transfers

For diaspora users booking from outside Ghana:

  • Data may be processed in your country of residence
  • Service delivery data is processed in Ghana
  • All transfers comply with international data protection standards
  • Adequate safeguards are in place for cross-border data transfers

10. Children's Privacy

HomeLinkGH is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:

  • Notify users of material changes via the app
  • Post updated policies on our website
  • Provide 30 days notice for significant changes
  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
